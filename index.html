About Us

Careers lingo expands your imagination on what careers you can pursue so that you can take up spaces, especially the ones that seem impossible to achieve.

We enable you to feel confident in mapping your career pathway.

  • We have a wide portfolio of careers to choose from, from traditional to modern careers
  • We use captivating stories of real people who have pursued the respective career paths and offer practical guidance — no motivation talk here.
  • We provide multiple pathways and education options - not just degrees - to help you explore and pursue your desired career.

Whether you're discovering careers for the first time or refining your choices, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Products and Services

Career Explorer Web App

Explore diverse career options and get insights into how careers are evolving across different career paths

Careers and Technology Handbook

A visually engaging resource designed to simplify career exploration and insights into emerging tech trends. For students, the handbook provides step-by-step guides on education pathways, tools, and technologies relevant to various industries. For teachers, it serves as an accessible guide for teachers to enhance career guidance sessions.

Career Cards

The Career Flash Cards are an innovative tool designed to make career exploration fun, interactive, and educational.The cards are designed to be used in classrooms, workshops, or group activities, encouraging learners to explore careers interactively.
